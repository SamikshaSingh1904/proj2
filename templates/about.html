This is the alpha version of clump!

clump aims to strengthen community ties by providing a platform that allows users to find clumps of people based on shared interests or needs. clump attempts to address recurring needs on campus, such as students needing to carpool for various reasons (to and from concert venues, grocery runs, traveling to the airport), students looking for hobby buddies (such as a gym partner at a given time), or students needing general help (plant-sitting or pet-sitting over short breaks) to name a few.
